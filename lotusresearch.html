Lotus Biotech is committed to translating science into solutions. We have completed numerous peer-reviewed, evidence-based clinical trials and translational research studies:

  • 1) Citrus bergamia Supplement Trial
    Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial on cholesterol & body weight in older adults with dyslipidemia (Lipids in Health and Disease, 2017, :251)
  • 2) BrainPower Memory Supplement Study
    12-week RCT on safety & efficacy in older adults with subjective memory complaints (Evidence-Based Complementary and Alternative Medicine, 2016)
  • 3) Omega-3 PUFA Study
    Marine Omega-3 PUFA supplementation for prehypertension/hypercholesterolemia (Lipids in Health and Disease, 2017)
  • 4) Polyherbal Glucose Control Trial
    RCT on fasting and postprandial glucose in prediabetic elderly (Evidence-Based Complementary and Alternative Medicine, 2019)
  • 5) Polyherbal Blood Pressure Trial
    RCT in older adults with prehypertension/hypertension (Evidence-Based Complementary and Alternative Medicine, 2019)
  • 6) Colonvita GI Relief Study
    RCT examining GIQLI improvement in seniors with chronic GI symptoms (Evidence-Based Complementary and Alternative Medicine, 2021)
  • 7) Neurohormonal & Inflammatory Biomarker Study
    Comparative analysis in schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and MDD (Journal of Psychiatric Research, 2021)
  • 8) Neuroprotection in Animal Model of Demyelination
    Effects of herbal decoction in mice (Neural Plasticity, 2021)
  • 9) Phytotherapy in TBI Recovery
    RCT testing Yunanan Baiyao and Xingnaojing (Phytomedicine, 2021)
  • 10) Amino Acids as Diabetes Biomarkers
    Branched/aromatic amino acids as T2D predictors (World Journal of Diabetes, 2024)
